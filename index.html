What is Oliver?

Oliver creates a more interactive and engaging experience between a Twitch streamer and a Twitch viewer. Viewers can level up and be rewarded by participating in your streaming events. Oliver allows for Twitch streamers to really bridge the connection and enjoy their gaming experiences together.

Reward your loyal fans or earn rewards

Streamers:

Award your loyal fans through Project Oliver, allowing you to setup giveaways, award currency for viewers and setup your own competitions.

Viewers:

Earn rewards by supporting your favourite players streams, the more time spent watching the more currency you earn, that curreny can then be spent on prizes!

Social sharing with subscribers

Epic Communication with the Chatbot

Setting up a chatbot with Oliver will allow you to instantly reward your viewers, giving you more time to focus on creating more high level plays that your viewers love.

Sharing all your content from one centralized place!

Oliver allows for you to integrate all your social gaming into one place, making it easy to update all your followers at once or tell your friends about an upcoming giveaway!
